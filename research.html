Research Focus

My research focuses on brain network analysis, graph theory, and machine learning. I am interested in understanding how structural and functional brain data can be used to study neurological conditions and improve classification models.

Current Work

I have worked with graph-based features extracted from brain connectivity data, including measures such as degree, clustering, and efficiency. I use these features in machine learning pipelines to study diagnosis-related patterns in neurological data.

Research Interests

  • Brain Network Analysis
  • Neurodegenerative Disease Research
  • Data-Driven Healthcare Applications

Why It Matters

I am interested in research that can contribute to meaningful real-world impact, especially in areas where data science and computer science can support healthcare and scientific understanding.
